## Long Description (full product page)

```
The Green Tomato has been the vegetable of record for Calhoun County, Alabama since recently. We cover what local boosterism cannot, including but not limited to: ribbon cuttings, wellness checks of museum exhibits, and the steady, ongoing development of the McClellan corridor.

This tote carries that same commitment in heavyweight canvas. It is suitable for groceries, library books, the contents of one's gear-check bag, and the kind of folding chairs that occasionally see use as foreign objects at the Downtown Anniston Farmers Market.

Printed in two colors — yellow on navy, with a single red ticker line. Holds approximately one (1) Hawaiian Punch jug, three (3) packages of off-brand sandwich cookies, and several (5+) regrets.

Heavy 12oz canvas. 14"w × 16"h × 3" gusset. Reinforced handles.

Printed by Fourthwall.

Slogan unchanged since slogan was selected. We do not anticipate changes.
```
