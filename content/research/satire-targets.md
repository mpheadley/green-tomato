The natural institutional rival for The Green Tomato. Buford covers the same beats. The Star has a staff photographer (Trent Wickliffe, already named in the raptor-run companion piece as "injured"). Angles:
- **Buford quotes the Star as a primary source** — he treats them as peer journalists, cites them earnestly in stories where they got it right. The Green Tomato covers the Star covering things.
- **Star publishes correction; Buford publishes correction of the correction** — short item
- **Star wins Alabama Press Association award; Buford congratulates them** — in full, with institutional gravity, for a photo of a parking lot
